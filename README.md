# IBM's Cyber Security Quiz for Employees
## Introduction
Workplace secruity is becoming increasignly important, where every employee must keep a level of securtiy to ensure the company's details don't get leaked to competitors or even hackers who could steal money or ruin the ruputation of the company. Therefore this quiz is made for employees at IBM to check their understanding of security, and to record their answers so if they score low their manager can ensure they get the necessary education to keep IBM safe.
